What is STEAK?
Stake is a ERC20 token with staking function on the Binance Smart Chain. If you participate in staking and have some STAKE in your wallet, your STEAK will automatically stake over time.
To get your staked token, you or someone else have to click the butcher button.

Butcher Button
If you or someone else clicks on the butcher button, all staker get rewarded with their staked token. Staked token will only cashed out trough the butcher button.
